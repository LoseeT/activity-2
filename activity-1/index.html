Facts about the Devil's Tower

This website will give you a bit of info on
Devil's Tower. Here's a few info on the
Tower itself:

    Devil's Tower is a natural formation and not man made
    In the Movie: Close Encounters of the Third Kind featured Devil's Tower in the movie
    People can climb up Devil's Tower but need to register at the ranger station
    It was first called "bad god's tower" by indians then refered to as Devil's Tower later
    Devil's Tower is the first US National Monument

Those were a few facts about Devil's Tower
